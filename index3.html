Cristiano Ronaldo All Awards List

Portugal

  1. UEFA European Championship Runner-up in 2004
  2. UEFA EURO 2012 Bronze award

Manchester United

  1. Premier League titles in 2006-07, 2007-2008, 2008-2009
  2. UEFA Champions League: 2007-08 & Runner up in 2008-09
  3. Football League Cup: 2005-06, 2008-09
  4. FA Cup: 2003-04 & Runner up in 2004-06, 2006-07
  5. FA Community Shield: 2007
  6. FIFA Club World Cup: 2008

Real Madrid C.F.

  1. Spanish league [La Liga]: 2011-12
  2. UEFA Champions league: 2013-14
  3. Copa del Rey: 2010-11, 2013-14
  4. Supercopa de Espana: 2012 & Runner-ups in 2011
  5. UEFA Super Cup: 2014

  3. PFA Young player of the year: 2005-06, 2006-07, 2007-08, 2008-09
  4. PFA player of the year: 2006-07, 2007-08

  6. Premier league golden boot: 2007-08
  7. European Golden Boot: 2007-08, 2010-11, 2013-14
  8. UEFA Champions league top scorer: 2007-08
  9. FIFA Ballon d’Or: 2008, 2013
  10. Copa del Rey top scorer: 2010-11
  11. Trofeo Alfredo Di Stefano: 2011-12, 2012-13, 2013-14
  12. IFFHS World’s best top goal scorer: 2013
  13. La Liga Top goal scorer: 2010-11, 2013-14
  14. UEFA Euro Top scorer: 2012
  15. LFP most Valuable player: 2012-13.
  16. LFP Best Player: 2013-14
  17. LFP Best Forward: 2013-14
  18. LFP Best Goal: 2013-14
